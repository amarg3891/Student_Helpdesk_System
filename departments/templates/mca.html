"MASTER OF COMPUTER APPLICATIONS"

MCA isa master degree in Computer applications. It is a 3 year post-graduate course. The minimum criteria to apply for MCA is graduation from any recognized university and the candidate should have completed 10+2 with Mathematics. The course structure of MCA is divided in 6 semesters. In these 6 semesters, the students have to study at least 35 subjects like C programming, Object oriented programming, Multimedia and business administration, Operation Research, Operating system, Accounting and Finance Management, Software engineering, Microprocessor, Database, Discrete mathematics etc. The course focuses on developing and implementing software at system and application level.
