B.Com

"BACHLOR OF COMMERCE"

BCom is a 3-year undergraduate course which deals with principles of Accounting and Finance. Those who have completed their higher secondary education (10+2) from a recognized board with at least 50% academic aggregate (5% relaxation for reserved categories) are eligible to apply for this course. Students can pursue this undergraduate program in either regular or distance mode of education. Several colleges offering BCom in distance education include - IGNOU, SOL DU, TNOU, Annamalai University, etc. BCom admissions are generally fulfilled on the basis of marks scored by a candidate in 10+2, however, some colleges admit students to the course based on their marks in national or state level entrance exams like IPU CET, DUET, BHU, etc.
